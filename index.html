Abydos and Blue: The Best Cats in the World

Abydos and Blue: Origin

Abydos and Blue are 5 years apart. We got Abydos for Christmas when I was 6. For a while, he was happy being the only cat at our house. When we realized he was depressed, we got Blue. They quickly bonded and they got along. There are times when they fight each other and get on each other's nerves. Nevertheless, they appreciate and love each other.
